Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is FlyDream Air?
FlyDream Air is an airline offering domestic and international flights with lounges, memberships, and rewards.
How do I create an account?
Go to the signup page, enter a unique username and password, then follow the prompts to complete registration.
Where can I see my bookings?
Open your profile page to view and manage current and past bookings.

Lounges & Bookings

How do I book a lounge?
Visit the Explore page, choose a lounge, select a date and time, then confirm on the pay page.
Can I cancel a lounge booking?
Yes. Go to your profile, find the booking, and select Cancel booking. Cancellation windows may apply.
What amenities are included?
Amenities vary by lounge and may include Wi-Fi, showers, food and beverages, and quiet work areas. See each lounge page for details.

Payments & Points

How can I pay?
You can pay with a credit card or redeem points where available during checkout.
How do I earn points?
Earn points by booking lounges and eligible purchases. Points are shown on your profile page.
Do points expire?
Points expiration policies may vary. Check your profile or program terms for the current policy.
